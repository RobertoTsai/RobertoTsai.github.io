Maximum Single Selection Liability is the total amount of money, which any customer can bet on any single selection. After reaching the maximum amount, the player cannot bet any more on the certain outcome. The limit can be expired either by betting once the total amount or many times by smaller amounts. One client can bet on single selection as much as selection liability, event limit, client limit allows.

Example

Client has placed a bet on Liverpool vs Everton event on the selection W1. The Game limit and partner selection liability allows to bet on this selection 10.000 USD. Client can bet on this selection once with max bet (10.000 USD) and he can place a bet with different amounts, but after reaching 10.000 USD he cant place bet on this selection any more until the delay time ends which has been set on global limits (Reset Player limit after minutes).

How is it calculated? Example

Selection liability is set 10.000 USD, Game limit is 10 %, the client, event market limits are 100

%, and the selection odd is 1.8.

For selection liability calculation, the following formula is used.

(10.000 USD X 10 %/100) X (100 % / 100) X (100 % / 100) / (1.8 1) = 1250 USD

The max bet of the selection of the example mentioned above will be 1250 USD.

Maximum Payout - The maximal amount, which is possible to be won by a single player for one coupon. Maximum Payout is the gross win that a client can receive on a coupon (exception Super Bets).


Min Stake the minimal stake for one bet for pre-match or live events. The client will not be able to place a bet with fewer amount than the value, which set as Min Stake.


PreMatch Max Bet Stake - maximal stake for one bet for pre-match event. This setting refers to one coupon. The client can place as many Max Stakes, as the Selection Liability (Limit) allows. After reaching limit, the client cannot place bet on this selection any more until the delay time ends.



2


Live Max Bet Stake - maximal stake for one bet for live event. This setting refers to one coupon. The client can place as many Max Stakes, as the Selection Liability (Limit) allows. After reaching limit, the client cannot place bet on this selection any more until the delay time ends.


Live Selection Limit is the default client live limit, which is calculated from Selection Liability. If there are not any values set, the default value is 100%.


PreMatch Selection Limit is the default client pre-match limit, which is calculated from Selection Liability. If there are not any values set, the default value is 100%.

There is an option, which allows us to set individual Selection limits depending on clients sportsbook profile. For example, VIP clients can get default 150% Selection Limits, while Arbitrage Betting Clients can get 5% default Selection Limits.


Max odd for multiple bet the maximal possible odd for multiple bet. Generally, it is set 1000, and no matter the event count and odds of the selections in the bet, the total odd cannot be higher that the value is set for Max odd for multiple bet section.


Selection Count in Multiple Bet the maximal possible event combination for multiple bets. If this section is set 50, the client will not be possible combine 51 or more events in one bet.




3



Reset Player Limits After minutes an option, which allows resetting clients limits (from selection liability) after the individual set time (minutes) for Live and Prematch.

Example

If the match is in prematch, and the client has expired its limit, player can repeat the bet and place a bet on a selection only after 10 minutes. If the match is in live after 2 minutes.

Configuration reset time (e.g. live - 2min., prematch - 10min.) can be set up based on Clients Sportsbook Profile. For High Risk clients can be used 20 min. in live and 60 min. in prematch, for VIP clients 1 min. in live and 3 min. in prematch.


Global live delay is an option, which supplement interval (seconds) after clicking place bet and bet acceptance actual time. This helps to prevent the cases, when a client can see the event earlier (e.g. from the stadium), than it is shown on the website.

There is an option, which allows us to set individual Live Delay time depending on clients sportsbook profile.

Example

For VIP clients can be set default negative -2 seconds live delay, which will help them to place bets faster without additional loading time. Meanwhile, for Late bet clients can get additional +5 seconds live delay, which will reduce the risk of Late bets on the event.

Manage Populars an option, which allows highlighting popular competitions from all other competitions, accordingly making it easier for clients to find most popular competitions from the list.


Just select the sport and region of desired competition at the top left corner of the page, and then click Apply. After that, all the competitions from selected region will appear. Press on the check mark under Popular section and put the order, in which you want it to be visible on the website.

As a result, the selected competitions will be visible under Popular competitions section above all other competitions and sports.

Market Type Booking an option, which allows deactivating certain market for any sport or any specific competition.


Simply choose Live or Pre-Match, then choose the sport and press on the edit icon on the right side. Press on the check mark under Unsubscribe section and for markets which you want to be unsubscribed and press on the save butted.

You can also use search field on the left side to simplify work.


The same view on the right side will help you to unsubscribe a certain market type for specific competitions.

Different sports in different regions differ in the interests of their clients and in the number of bets. The same sport can be very popular in Asia, but at the same time generate less interest in the European market. The same applies to different championships in a particular sport, where in one country the domestic championship is more important than the same championship for the clients of another partner. The logic of the markets is not excluded from this logic, some of which are more important and require higher betting limits than others.

In this case, it is very important to classify sports, championships, markets according to their priorities in order to give customers the opportunity to place higher bets on them. Bet Limits Sport Limits gives this possibility.


From you Backoffice account open Sportsbook Management Bet Limits Sport Limits section. If you want to change Sport Limits, press Apply button, and then Edit button from the top right corner. Insert limit percentage and press save.

Inserted value is a percentage from Selection Liability (see page 1).



7


If you want to change the limits of specific competition or market Type, from Filter section choose Sport, Region, then press Apply. Repeating the same process as for Sport limits you can change the limits here as well.
